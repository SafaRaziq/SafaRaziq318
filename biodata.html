About Me

My full name is Safa Muhammad Raziq Bin Abd Rafar, but most people know me simply as Safa. I was born in Tanjong Karang Hospital, which is where my journey began. Growing up, I was fortunate to be raised in a close-knit family environment in our hometown of Tanjong Karang, Selangor, where I still live with my parents.

In my family, I am the middle child of three siblings, which has given me a unique perspective, balancing both the responsibility of being an older sibling and learning from my older sibling. Family has always been a significant part of my life, and our bond is something I deeply value.

An interesting part of my background is that I also spent a portion of my childhood in Kulaijaya, Johor. Living there provided me with different experiences and broadened my understanding of life in Malaysia, shaping me in many ways.
